Our Signature Blends

Arsino de Paris

Arsino is a Citrus Aromatic fragrance for women with top nodes of Grapefruit, Lavender, Mint and Bergamot. Middle notes are Granny Smith apple, Cranberry and Rose. Base notes are Cotton Flower, Cactus and Amber.

Vermont

Vermont is a Sweet Aromatic fragrance for women with top nodes of Grapefruit, Lavender, Mint and Bergamot. Middle notes are Granny Smith apple, Cranberry and Rose. Base notes are Cotton Flower, Cactus and Amber.
